About Mandric Biocare

Our story, mission, values and the journey of 8+ years in healthcare.

Who We Are
Dedicated to Your Wellbeing

Mandric Biocare Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted pharmaceutical company dedicated to quality healthcare. Established in 2017 with a mission to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable, we have grown over 8+ years to serve thousands of patients, doctors, pharmacies and hospitals across the region.

We specialize in Pharmaceutical, Surgical, Paediatric and Orthopaedic products — covering 700+ products across 15+ therapeutic specialities. Our commitment: quality products, honest pricing, and reliable service.

"Our goal is your wellbeing. We sell all types of products covering your health, beauty and well-being. We stand behind all the products we sell — quality and affordability, always."
